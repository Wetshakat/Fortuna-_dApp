# Fortuna

Fortuna: Digital Lottery Platform
Tagline: Play Fair. Win Big. Anytime.

1. Overview
Fortuna is a next-generation digital lottery platform designed for fairness, transparency, and excitement.
Players can join lottery rounds to compete for a single prize pool, with rounds automatically managed to
ensure efficiency.
Each round accommodates up to 50 players.
New rounds open automatically as more participants join.
Rounds complete when full or after a set time, keeping the game moving.

2. The Problem
Trust issues in traditional lotteries – Players must rely on centralized organizations to pick winners
fairly.
Limited participation options – Traditional lotteries can leave users waiting if rounds don’t fill
quickly.
Inflexible participation – Fixed entry requirements or waiting periods frustrate players, reducing
engagement.

3. Our Solution
Fair and Transparent: Every round is impartial and winners are reliably selected.
Flexible: Rounds complete based on player count or time, preventing funds from being locked and
keeping players engaged.
User-Friendly: Simple interface to join, track rounds, and view winners.
Automatic Management: New rounds are created automatically when previous rounds end,
keeping gameplay continuous.

 4. How Users Participate
1.Players join a round by paying the entry fee.
2. Once the round reaches 50 players or time expires, a winner is selected.
3. The winner receives the entire prize pool minus a platform fee.
4. A new round opens automatically for additional participants.
5. If no one joins a round, the entry is refunded automatically.

5. Revenue Model
Platform Fee: Small percentage of each prize pool (e.g., 2%) – revenue grows as more rounds and
players participate.
Premium Rounds: VIP slots or early access with higher entry fees.
Sponsorship Opportunities: Brands sponsor rounds with additional prizes for exposure.
Revenue grows as more rounds are played and higher stakes are used.

6. Market Opportunity
Online lottery market is growing globally, attracting users seeking fair, convenient, and exciting
digital lotteries.
Fortuna targets users looking for trustworthy, instant, and fun gaming experiences.
Automatic round management allows continuous participation without delays.


8. Key Highlights
1. Fair, transparent, and automatic lottery rounds
2. Each round accommodates up to 50 players
3. Revenue from every round with optional premium features
4. Flexible completion rules prevent delays or locked funds
5. Simple, intuitive interface encourages repeated participation
